Sam's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time

A Few Good Men

Tom Cruise defends two Marines who followed orders that resulted in the death of another Marine.

Remember the Titans

Denzell Washington coaches a football team on the heals of high schools integrating in the South.

Goodwill Hunting

An orphan, Matt Damon, who proves to be a mathematical sevant develops a relationship with his court mandated therapist, Robin Williams, in which they both learn something about themselves and achieve a deeper understanding of the world outside of south Boston
